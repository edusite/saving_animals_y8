What can we do

To save the animals?

There are lots of ways to save the animals. We have provided some tips that we all can do! Let's cooperate and give it a try!





Pick up trash

Target group - marine animals

This is one of the most effective ways in saving marine animals since trash has become the biggest issue of water pollution.




Adopt an animal

Target group - Wild and stray animals

Adopting means you can saving one of their lives! Not only you save a life but also obtain the companion.




Donate

Target group - wild,stray and marine animals

Donating can be a support for the animals because your donations help to maintain these vital conservation areas and provide them food.




Volunteer

Target group - wild and stray

If you don’t have money to give, donate your time. In some of the organizations, there can be lack of employees so, volunteering will not only help them but also a way of maintaining the animals.




Speak up

Target group - wild, stray and marine animals

Spread awareness by sharing your passion for wildlife conservation and the ways of helping to your family and friends.




Join organizations

Target group - Wildlife and stray animals

Whether you’re into protecting natural habitats or preventing wildlife trafficking, find the organization that speaks to your passion and get involved.




Buy responsibly

Target group - wildlife and marine

Most of the animals are endangered because of human's greed. So, try not to purchase products made from endangered animals or their parts




Restore

Target group - wildlife and marine

Habitat destruction is the main threat to 85 percent of all threatened and endangered species, according to the International Union for Conservation of Nature. You can help reduce this threat by planting native trees, restoring wetlands or cleaning up beaches in your area.
